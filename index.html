Transferring Data from Previous TurboTax Versions

  1. Open TurboTax: Launch the TurboTax software on your computer.
  2. Sign In: Use your Intuit account credentials to sign in.
  3. Select Transfer Data: Go to the "Tax Home" screen, then select "Tax Tools" and "Tools." From there, choose "Transfer last year’s TurboTax return from your computer."
  4. Follow Instructions: TurboTax will guide you through the steps to locate and import your previous tax data file (.tax file) from your computer.
  5. Verify Data: Ensure that all relevant information, such as personal details, income, deductions, and credits, has been successfully transferred.
